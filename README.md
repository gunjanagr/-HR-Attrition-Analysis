# HR Attrition Analysis

23% of employees left. Sales Representatives quit at 40%. 
Overtime workers are 3x more likely to leave.

## Business Questions Answered
- Which departments have the highest attrition?
- Which job roles are most at risk?
- Does overtime drive attrition?
- When in their tenure do employees quit?

## Key Findings
- Sales has the highest attrition rate (21%) across all departments
- Sales Representatives quit at 40% — highest of any role
- Overtime workers leave at 30% vs 10% for non-overtime — 3x difference
- Highest attrition in year 0-2 — onboarding is the biggest retention failure

## Business Recommendations
1. Cap overtime immediately — it is the single biggest controllable attrition driver
2. Investigate Sales Representative compensation — 40% attrition is unsustainable
3. Build a 90-day onboarding program — most people leave in first 2 years
4. Salary review for HR and Lab Technician roles — both above 20% attrition
